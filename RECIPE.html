RECIPE OF UGALI AND BEEF


ingredients

  • maize flour
  • water
  • two onions
  • three tomatoes
  • cooling oil
  • garlic
  • green pepper
  • black pepper
  • curry powder

    • PROCEDURE FOR MAKING BEEF
  • STEP 1:Heat oil in a heavy ased pot.Add onions and stir. Cook for 30 seconds until translucent
  • STEP 2:Stir in the garlic and curry powder.Add the beef cubes, salt,pepper,tomatoes ,black pepper,green pepper and some water then stir
  • STEP 3:Cover the pot with a lid and let simmer for 30-40 minutes Garnish with coriander and set aside while you prepare the ugali.

    • FOR MAKING UGALI
  • STEP 1:Bring the water to boil in a pot.Add 1\2 cup of maize flour to the boiling water.Adding a little bit of the flour to the water ensures that the water starts to roar which makes the perfect ugali.Stir using a wooden spatula and try to break any lumps apart
  • STEP 2:Once cooked mould it using the spatula and serve with a serving of the beef stew that you had cooked earlier
